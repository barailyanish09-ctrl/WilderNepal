Our Story

About Wilder Nepal

Hi, I'm Priya. Welcome to my Nepal.

Wilder Nepal started as a collection of trail notes scribbled in a beaten notebook somewhere between Namche Bazaar and Dingboche. It grew into this — a space to share every raw, beautiful, difficult, and transcendent moment Nepal has given me.

I'm a Kathmandu-based writer and avid trekker with ten years of exploring Nepal's mountains, jungles, and cultural valleys. I believe in slow travel, authentic experiences, and telling stories that go beyond the postcard.

This blog is for curious souls who want the real Nepal — not a curated package tour, but a genuine adventure.
